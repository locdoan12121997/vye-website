The theme of VYE 2018 is the Ultimate Business Master Class.

Vietnam is a very different place from when we started VYE in 2011. Vietnam is one of the strongest growing countries to watch for in 2018. We are now seeing interest from foreign investors in Vietnam at its greatest extent. There is an abundance of opportunities waiting for young people to grab.

Our 3-day most comprehensive boot camp yet is where business thought leaders and trainers will join force in putting together the latest tools of the trade, insights and wisdoms to prepare our future entrepreneurs with rock-solid understanding of what it takes to start and run a successful international business from Vietnam.

Over the last 7 years, VYE boot camps have grown to become a trusted training platform for passionate, young Vietnamese entrepreneurs. Many of our alumni have already found massive success in starting and running their own companies, while many others have gone on to leadership positions in international corporations. Here we highlight some of the successful founders from our previous boot camps who are making their mark not only in Vietnam but also in the world.

From there, we can uncover ways that self-understanding would be the foundation on which you can look for the right partner for your startup. Once we uncover the unique ways you can find your partner, we will discover how to scale your business. This very skill is vital in running a successful venture and it will prove to be more relevant as you scale up your team from 5 people to 100 people. Make no mistake about scaling as failure in doing so will result in an out of control organization. There are a lot of myths into building the right team such as, "Get the A person on the bus", "What we all care about when investing in startup is THE TEAM". Therefore, you must learn to navigate effectively the path of sustainable and market-viable innovation that will allow you to consistently deliver value to your customers day in and day out. You will be presented with case studies of different startups approaching the same problem with different innovative solutions, which will serve as your map to innovating your way to the next product that customers will love.[/tg_accordion] [tg_accordion title="Day 3 August 26th: Compelling Sale Pitch" icon="plus" close="1"]In the startup world, product & sale go hand in hand. The challenging part of making your great sale pitch is that you need to sell the future of your company.
